About Us

Hero Express is Drayage Trucking Company, based in Downey, CA. We provide freight transportation for dry and reefer containers out of ports of Los Angeles/ Long Beach.

Hero Expressway incorporated in November 2021, providing the transportation of full container loads throughout Southern California.We believe in efficient and professional services. With us at your service, your shipments are in safe hands and we strive to deliver them on time.

Our Team dedication is to providing our clients safe, fast, and high-quality transportation services, the reason we have maintained a reputation for superior customer service and satisfaction. With our industry expertise providing services for freight forwarders, customs brokers, airlines, private firms, and other trucking companies, we welcome a diverse range of clients and freight.
