Intermodel Frieght Transportation

Intermodal freight transport is defined as the use of two or more modes to move a shipment from origin to destination. An intermodal movement involves the physical infrastructure, goods movement and transfer, and information drivers and capabilities under a single freight bill

24/7 Container Yard storage

Hero Express has a secured storage for container which is distant to less than 5 miles from the port and its terminals. Our Yard is fully secured under 24/7 CCTV surveillance in order to avoid any unwanted trespassing, 4 corner fencing, locking at the enternace/ exit gate. If there would be need to park your reefer container until the next FRD then you can have powered your reefers in our yard as well.
